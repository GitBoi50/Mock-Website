Recipe For A Cheese Pizza

This recipe is a bit more basic than the others.

Ingredients for all Steps

1 1/2 cups Bisquick™ Original baking mix
1/3 cup very hot water
1 can (8 ounces) pizza sauce
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese (6 ounces)

Cooking Instructions:

1.Move oven rack to lowest position. Heat oven to 450°. Grease 12-inch pizza pan. Stir baking mix and water until soft dough forms.
2.Pat dough in pizza pan, using fingers dipped in baking mix; pinch edge, forming 1/2-inch rim. Spread pizza sauce over crust. Sprinkle with cheese.
3.Bake 12 to 15 minutes or until crust is brown and cheese is bubbly.
This yields: 1 pizza, of about 4 slices.

And now you have a delicious cheese pizza!
